Consolee NISINGIZWE

"Turning uncertainty into insight through mathematics and data."

I am a graduate student pursuing a Master's degree in Mathematical Sciences (Data Science) at the African Institute for Mathematical Sciences (AIMS) Rwanda. My academic journey began with a Bachelor's degree in Physics, specializing in Atmospheric and Climate Sciences from the University of Rwanda.

My research interests lie at the intersection of statistical machine learning, theoretical statistics, and their applications to climate and environmental data. I am passionate about leveraging mathematical rigor and computational methods to extract meaningful insights from complex, uncertain systems.

Through my work, I aim to bridge the gap between theoretical foundations and practical applications, contributing to data-driven solutions for real-world challenges.
